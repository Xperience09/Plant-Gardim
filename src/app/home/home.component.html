Plants used for decorating your house

As the nights grow longer, use plants to create a peaceful atmosphere in your bedroom. Rapunzel the golden pothos and other hanging plants give interest at various heights and don't mind a little shadow. Both Pippa the peace lily and Susie the snake plant have remarkable structures and release oxygen at night, making them both useful and attractive.

Fill up vacant spaces with a tall, thin plant, such as Silvy the satin pothos on a mosspole or Phil the philodendron scandens. Maximum effect with minimal floorspace.

Don't forget to bring your pots. Matching neutral pots will appear clean and beautiful, whilst a combination of designs and patterns will appear diverse and exciting.
